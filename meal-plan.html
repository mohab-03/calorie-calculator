How to Build a Meal Plan

🧠 Step 1: Know Your Calories
First, calculate your TDEE to determine how many calories you need daily. Whether your goal is to lose, maintain, or gain weight – calories are king.

🍗 Step 2: Set Your Macros
Split your calories into protein, carbs, and fats. A good starting point: 30% protein, 40% carbs, 30% fats. Adjust based on your activity level, preference, and goals.

🍽️ Step 3: Choose Whole Foods
Build meals using nutrient-dense foods:

  • Proteins: chicken, beef, fish, eggs, Greek yogurt
  • Carbs: rice, potatoes, oats, fruits, vegetables
  • Fats: olive oil, avocado, nuts, seeds

🕐 Step 4: Plan Meal Timing
Distribute meals evenly throughout the day. 3–5 meals with protein in each one helps with satiety and muscle repair.

📦 Step 5: Prep & Track
Prep meals in advance and track your food intake using apps like MyFitnessPal. Consistency beats perfection!

🔁 Bonus Tip
Be flexible! You can enjoy occasional treats – just make sure they fit your daily macros. This is called Flexible Dieting.
