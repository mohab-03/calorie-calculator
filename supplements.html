Supplements Explained

Essential Supplements 💊

  • Protein Powder: Helps meet daily protein needs easily.
  • Creatine Monohydrate: Boosts strength and muscle mass. Safe and well-researched.
  • Omega-3 (Fish Oil): Supports heart, brain, and joints.
  • Multivitamin: Covers daily micronutrient gaps.
  • Vitamin D3: Especially important if you don’t get enough sun.
  • Magnesium: Helps with sleep, muscle recovery, and energy production.
  • Zinc: Supports immunity and hormone health.

Optional but Helpful 🚀

  • Pre-Workout: Enhances focus and energy before training.
  • BCAAs / EAAs: May support recovery during intense sessions (less needed if total protein is high).
  • Ashwagandha: May reduce stress and improve performance.
  • Collagen: Supports joint, skin, and tendon health.
