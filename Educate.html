Nutrition Education 🍎

🔥 Calories: Calories fuel your body. To lose fat, eat fewer calories. To gain muscle, eat more!

💪 Protein: Essential for muscle repair and growth. Aim for about 1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight.

🥦 Carbohydrates: Your body's energy source. Include whole grains, fruits & veggies.

🥑 Fats: Important for hormones & brain health. Don’t fear healthy fats!

⏰ Consistency: The key to progress. Stick to your plan and be patient.

📊 BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate): This is the amount of energy your body needs at rest to keep vital functions running.

⚡ TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure): This includes your BMR plus all physical activities and exercise — it’s your total daily calorie burn.

⚖️ Calorie Deficit: Eating fewer calories than your TDEE to lose fat.

➕ Calorie Surplus: Eating more calories than your TDEE to gain muscle.
